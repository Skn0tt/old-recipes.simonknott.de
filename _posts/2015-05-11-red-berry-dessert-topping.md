date:   2015-01-15 16:26:20
categories: components
image: red-berry-topping.jpg

ingredients:
- 1/2 cup raspberries
- 1/2 cup strawberries
- 1/4 white sugar
- 1 squirt honey


directions:
- Dice berries
- Combine all ingredients
- Let rest for 15 mins

---

A simple mash of berries, perfect for a tart or top of cheesecake.
