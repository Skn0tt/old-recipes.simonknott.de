title:  "Crockpot Buffalo Chicken"
date:   2015-02-25 16:26:20
categories: meals
image: crockpot.jpg

ingredients:
- 6-8 chicken breasts
- 1 packet ranch mix
- 1 bottle Frank's Red Hot
- 1 stick of butter

directions:
- Add all the above into a crockpot, cook on low for 6-8 hours
- Shred chicken when soft
- Serve with pickles and Hawaiian rolls
